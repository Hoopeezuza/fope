Fried in Ben10: A Sci-Fi Snack

Ingredients

  • 4 large potatoes (cut into fries)
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika (for that fiery alien spice!)
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of green food coloring (for the Omnitrix glow)
  • Futuristic dipping sauce (recipe below!)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. In a large bowl, toss the fries with olive oil, garlic powder, paprika, and salt. Add a few drops of green food coloring for that extra Ben10 flair.
  3. Spread the fries in a single layer on a baking sheet. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until crispy and golden brown.
  4. While the fries are baking, make your futuristic dipping sauce: Combine mayo, mustard, and a dash of green food coloring. Stir until the sauce takes on an otherworldly hue!
  5. Once the fries are out of the oven, serve them with the dipping sauce and enjoy the alien-inspired goodness!

Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients

  • 1 lb (450g) ground beef
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup red wine (optional)
  • 1/4 cup milk or cream
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 lb spaghetti
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (for serving)

Instructions

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium heat. Add the ground beef and cook until browned, breaking it up with a spoon as it cooks.
  2. Add the chopped onion and garlic to the pan and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  3. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, red wine (if using), milk, oregano, and basil. Bring to a simmer and cook for 20-30 minutes, until the sauce thickens. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Meanwhile, cook the spaghetti according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  5. Serve the spaghetti topped with the Bolognese sauce and a generous sprinkle of Parmesan cheese.
