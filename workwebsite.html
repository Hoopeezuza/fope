üçù Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup red wine (optional)
  • 1/4 cup milk or cream
  • Oregano, basil, salt, pepper
  • 1 lb spaghetti
  • Grated Parmesan

Instructions

  1. Brown beef in skillet, add onion and garlic.
  2. Add tomatoes, wine, milk, and seasonings. Simmer 30 mins.
  3. Cook spaghetti. Drain.
  4. Serve with sauce and cheese.
